OUR MISSION

World-class expertise
Cutting-edge innovation

Eir is built by a team of elite athletes, experienced coaches, human physiology experts, and product developers from leading tech and design firms. We understand performance inside and out—because we live it.

​

Our origins trace back to GRASPOR, a muscle oxygen monitoring technology initially developed to support astronaut fitness in space. In 2023, Eir acquired GRASPOR to bring this groundbreaking technology to endurance athletes and fitness-driven individuals worldwide.

 

Today, we are delivering a world-class training platform designed for those who are determined to push their limits and make the most of their body and time.

​

Eir Technology is named after Eir (air), the Norse goddess of healing and endurance, reflecting our mission to optimize human performance and recovery through cutting-edge wearable technology.
